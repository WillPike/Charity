HUNGER RELIEF CAUSE FUND

Worldwide, more than 800 million people struggle with hunger every day. That number has dropped significantly over the past two decades thanks to the painstaking effort of individuals, nonprofits and NGOs alike, but there is much work to be done. Your donation can make a difference. Our Hunger Relief Cause Fund includes our charity members, fully vetted by America's Charities and focused on outcomes-based impact, working toward ending hunger in the U.S. and around the world.

  • Generous organizations provide food assistance to millions of people facing hunger;
  • connect kids in need with nutritious and nourishing food;
  • teach families how to cook healthy, affordable meals;
  • advocate for public policies that positively impact hungry Americans; and much more.
